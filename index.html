BomDesign Publisher

An internal multi-channel publishing tool that posts our own video content to the social accounts we own — TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, and Threads — from one place.

What it does

BomDesign Publisher takes a video we produced and publishes it to our owned social media accounts in a single workflow. For TikTok, it uses the Content Posting API to upload and directly post the video to our own account, with our caption and chosen privacy setting.

BomDesign Publisher는 자사가 제작한 영상을 자사 소유 SNS 계정에 한 번에 발행하는 내부 도구입니다. TikTok의 경우 Content Posting API로 자사 계정에 영상을 업로드·직접 게시합니다.

First-party only

Posts only our own content to accounts we own. No third-party or user-generated content.

One workflow

Publishes to TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, and Threads together.

Direct Post

Uploads and direct-posts to our TikTok account using video.upload and video.publish.

Internal use

Operated only by our authorized staff — not offered to the public.

TikTok integration

Scopes used: user.info.basic, video.upload, video.publish. The tool authenticates our own TikTok account and posts our marketing videos to it. It does not access other users' data.
