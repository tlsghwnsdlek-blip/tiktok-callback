Privacy Policy

BomDesign Publisher  ·  Last updated: June 9, 2026

BomDesign Publisher ("the App", "we", "us") is an internal multi-channel publishing tool operated by Onsale / BomDesign. The App publishes our own self-produced video content to social media accounts that we own — including our own TikTok account — alongside YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, and Threads. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses and how we use it.

BomDesign Publisher는 온세일/봄Design이 운영하는 내부 멀티채널 발행 도구로, 자사가 소유한 SNS 계정(자사 TikTok 계정 포함)에 자체 제작 영상을 발행합니다. 본 방침은 앱이 접근하는 정보와 그 사용 방법을 설명합니다.

1. Scope of this Policy

The App is used only by our own staff to post first-party marketing content to accounts we own. The App does not have public end users, does not collect content or data from other TikTok users, and does not post third-party or user-generated content.

본 앱은 자사 직원만 사용하며, 자사 소유 계정에 자사 콘텐츠를 발행하는 용도입니다. 일반 외부 이용자가 없으며, 다른 TikTok 이용자의 데이터를 수집하거나 제3자 콘텐츠를 게시하지 않습니다.

2. Information We Access

Through the TikTok API and the permissions (scopes) we request, the App accesses the following, strictly for our own account:

TikTok scopeWhat it accessesWhy
user.info.basicOur own account's basic profile (open id, avatar, display name)To confirm which of our accounts we are publishing to
video.uploadUploads our rendered video file to our own account (as a draft)To prepare our content for posting
video.publishDirect-posts our video to our own account with our caption and privacy settingTo publish our content without a manual step

We do not access other users' profiles, followers, private messages, or any personal data of third parties.

위 권한은 모두 자사 계정에 한해 사용되며, 다른 이용자의 프로필·팔로워·메시지·개인정보에는 접근하지 않습니다.

3. How We Use the Information

  • To authenticate our own TikTok account and upload/publish our own videos.
  • To display which account a video was posted to, within our internal tool.

We do not use the information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to publishing our own content.

4. Storage and Retention

The App stores only the access and refresh tokens needed to call the TikTok API on our own account. Tokens are kept in a secured internal credential store and are refreshed or revoked as needed. We retain them only while the App is in active use.

앱은 자사 계정 API 호출에 필요한 액세스/리프레시 토큰만 보안 저장소에 보관하며, 사용 기간 동안만 유지합니다.

5. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share any data obtained through the TikTok API with third parties. Data is used solely within our own publishing workflow.

6. Data Security

Access tokens and credentials are stored securely and are accessible only to authorized operators. Communication with TikTok is performed over encrypted HTTPS connections.

7. Your Rights / Revocation

Because the App operates only on our own accounts, you may revoke the App's access at any time from your TikTok account settings ("Manage app permissions"). Doing so immediately stops the App from accessing the account.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest revision.
